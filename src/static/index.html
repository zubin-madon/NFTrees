Important! Read before you sow!

  • Generate a tree made up of all the ERC721 tokenIds (leaves) and addresses (roots).
  • There are thousands of permutations viz a viz shape and colour.
  • If you see something you like, mint it! Chances are you won't see that unique combo again for hundreds of clicks.
  • Generating/ Minting your tree may take a while. Patience is a virtue and we're on a free plan.
  • As your NFT collection grows, keep coming back to mint and watch your wallet tree evolve.
  • There will be a MetaForest and gamified lumbering. You can't be kingpin of the timber mafia with just one tree!
